facebook.com

URL: https://www.facebook.com/

Visitor Analysis

Facebook seems to try and be accessible to all but is primarily aimed at the "millenials."
Ages 25-35 social individuals who like to be out doing things. It provides a great way for those people to
be with and plan events with their friends. It's organized to be navigated simply and information flows from most recent to oldest.
The repetition of the navigation bar at the top of the page made it very easy to navigate.
I think it did facilitate for good mobile browsing. It didn't require any pinch zooming was easily navigatable and I never clicked the wrong thing or ended up at the wrong page.

Persona

This is John. John works at a tech startup in california. He likes to stay indoors and enjoys dinner parties with his friends.
John uses Facebook to keep in touch with and plan activities with his group of friends from work.
